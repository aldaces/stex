STEX in Europe

We are proud to be one of the most trusted express logistics companies in Europe, offering immediate pick-up at the desired location, complete shipment tracking via GPS and safe direct deliveries within Germany and throughout Europe.

A network of over 7,000 vehicles helps us to ensure our clients productivity in an emergency and to transport goods of almost any size and shape from the pick-up place to the desired destination just in time.
